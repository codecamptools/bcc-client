    Boise Code Camp is celebrating 15 years!

    Saturday March 21, 2020 

    Welcome Code Camp Supporters

    Thank you for supporting the developer community over the past 15 years. Boise Code Camp is a free one-day event full of presentations for developers, hardware geeks, and the curious of all ages and skills to come and learn. This event is presented in partnership with the Computer Science Department at Boise State University.

    Our organization, Boise Code Camp Inc., is a Federal 501 (C)(3) non-profit and an Idaho non-profit. We only exist through the generosity of our volunteers and our sponsors each year. 

    We are excited to present our plans for Boise Code Camp this year, and with the help of your valued contribution we are aiming to raise a total of  $40,000 to fulfill our mission of elevating the awareness, education and overall technical skill of our community. 


    Why Boise Code Camp

    1) 15 Years of Community! We know who you need.

    2) Incredible registration and attendance over a 1,500 people each year for the past 5 years.

    3) Outstanding Guest Speakers from some of the largest tech companies in the world.

    4) Have we mentioned it is free to attend.

    5) The best place to find Senior tech talent in Boise.

    Who attends Code Camp?

    BOISE'S hidden trove of talent does

    Over the past decade, Boise has been experiencing an incredible influx of skilled workers looking for new jobs in the tech industry. These individuals are fleeing less affordable areas like Los Angeles, Portland, and Seattle. This population boom has earned Forbes’ number one spot in the U.S. this year for overall growth in 2018.

    Over 40% of Boise residents hold a Bachelor’s degree or higher, and the Boise School District’s graduation rate is 94.4%. Boise is also home to Boise State University, Idaho’s largest post-secondary institution.

    Year after year as we gather we speak geek with over 1,500 people.  We take special care to learn what this talent is looking for in their careers and we make lasting connections. These connections come in handy when your looking for a special skill because we just so happen to know that someone.

    The Benefits

    In the 21st century, Idaho needs software innovators and we have a responsibility to invest in the next generation of software developers. Software development is challenging and creative and it thrives in a vibrant community. Since 2005, Boise Code Camp has been building that community and we need your financial support. Our event has always been free to anyone interested in learning about technology and connecting with others. Please join us in supporting the software developer community in southern Idaho.
